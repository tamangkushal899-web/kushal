# NEB Grade 12 Social Studies Syllabus ## History 1. **Unit 1: Modern World History** - Chapter 1: The World Between The Wars - Chapter 2: The Second World War - Chapter 3: The Post-War World 2. **Unit 2: Contemporary World Issues** - Chapter 4: Globalization - Chapter 5: Human Rights - Chapter 6: Environmental Issues ## Geography 1. **Unit 1: Physical Geography** - Chapter 1: Earth’s Structure - Chapter 2: Climate and Weather - Chapter 3: Natural Resources 2. **Unit 2: Human Geography** - Chapter 4: Population and Migration - Chapter 5: Urbanization - Chapter 6: Development Issues ## Civics 1. **Unit 1: Citizenship and Governance** - Chapter 1: The Concept of Citizenship - Chapter 2: The Role of Government - Chapter 3: Political Parties and Elections 2. **Unit 2: Rights and Responsibilities** - Chapter 4: Civil Rights - Chapter 5: Civic Responsibilities - Chapter 6: Participation in Democracy --- This syllabus serves as a guide for students to understand the topics covered in Grade 12 Social Studies as per NEB standards.
