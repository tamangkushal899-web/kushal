NEB Grade 12 Mathematics Syllabus

1. Algebra

  • Real numbers and operations
  • Polynomials
  • Quadratic equations
  • Sequences and series
  • Functions and graphs

2. Geometry

  • Lines and angles
  • Triangles
  • Quadrilaterals
  • Circles
  • Area and volume

3. Trigonometry

  • Trigonometric ratios
  • Unit circle
  • Trigonometric identities
  • Graphs of trigonometric functions
  • Applications of trigonometry

4. Calculus

  • Limits and continuity
  • Differentiation
  • Integration
  • Applications of derivatives and integrals

5. Statistics

  • Data collection and analysis
  • Measures of central tendency
  • Probability
  • Distribution and representation of data
